A) Introduction - top

It is increasingly important nowadays, to focus on online presence for any retail solution out there. Niche online stores seldom make sales online, but it is imperative for essential product retailers (eg. Grocery Store Managers & Warehouse Owners) to expand their businesses using the unlimited virtual real eastate the internet has to offer. This is almost synonymous to increasing the amount of walk-in customers at your supermarket. So, if you are in the retail business or simply a representative and want to increase your outreach; we here at Designing Media bring you Groxi. A design template, made using Adobe Photoshop just for you.

Since immense time was invested in research and design thinking, Groxi is future-proof and takes into account usability considerations as well. You could use this PSD Template as the design foundation for your store's website, as a sales pitch, or even for inspiration. These layered and structured PSD files will help you focus on other more crucial departments of your retail business, whilst ensuring a pleasing end-user experience for your customers.
